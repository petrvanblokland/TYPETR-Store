Responder M

TYPETR Responder M — a fresh take on a famous classic. If you’ve ever admired the quirky charm of Mercator’s old handwriting, this one’s going to feel like an old friend with a new outfit. It’s based on the same broad nib pen strokes — like a sketchbook scribble with just the right touch of intention, a little rough around the edges, in the best possible way. Now with all the advantages of digital type, under the hood, there’s some OpenType magic going on.
