Presti

TYPETR Presti is a sophisticated serif typeface inspired by the flexible-pen contrast of traditional expansion stroke models, echoing the refined style of Bodoni with a modern twist. With its wide range of weights and optical sizes, Presti adapts beautifully across both print and digital media, from delicate captions to bold headlines. Rich in OpenType features, it offers a stunning array of stylistic alternates, flourishes, and typographic finesse, making it ideal for editorial design, branding, and luxury packaging.

Responder P

TYPETR Responder P captures the character of Petr’s handwriting, blending authenticity with typographic control. With a range of OpenType features, you can fine-tune its appearance—adjusting small caps, alternates, and connection levels to suit everything from casual notes to expressive branding. It’s handwriting, refined for design.

Proforma Pro

TYPETR Proforma Pro is a comprehensive redesign of the classic Proforma typeface, celebrating over 30 years of typographic excellence. Now completely renewed, it features an extended glyph set covering a wide spectrum of Latin-based languages. Enhanced with a rich array of OpenType features — including small caps, superior/inferior characters, and multiple figure styles — Proforma Pro brings timeless clarity and modern versatility to contemporary typography.

Proforma (Original) — A Landmark

Originally released by Font Bureau in the early 1990s, Proforma was a groundbreaking typeface that set new standards for digital text typography. Designed for clarity and efficiency in long-form reading, it combines classical proportions with a contemporary sensibility, balancing robust structure and subtle detail. Its carefully engineered spacing and intelligent use of contrast made it a favorite among typographers for editorial, corporate, and institutional use. Proforma remains a milestone in type design—timeless, readable, and remarkably forward-thinking. Also find the redesigned Proforma Pro.

Upgrade

TYPETR Upgrade is a refined sans-serif built on the subtle translation contrast of a broad-nib pencil, bringing warmth and rhythm to a clean, modern structure. Its extended glyph set supports a wide range of Latin-based languages, while advanced OpenType features—such as alternate forms, small caps, and multiple numeral styles—offer designers precise typographic control. Upgrade clarity with character.
