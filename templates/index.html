TYPETR Presti is a sophisticated serif typeface inspired by the flexible-pen contrast of traditional expansion stroke models, echoing the refined style of Bodoni with a modern twist. With its wide range of weights and optical sizes, Presti adapts beautifully across both print and digital media, from delicate captions to bold headlines. Rich in OpenType features, it offers a stunning array of stylistic alternates, flourishes, and typographic finesse, making it ideal for editorial design, branding, and luxury packaging. More...

Responder P

TYPETR Responder P captures the character of Petr’s handwriting, blending authenticity with typographic control. With a range of OpenType features, you can fine-tune its appearance—adjusting small caps, alternates, and connection levels to suit everything from casual notes to expressive branding. It’s handwriting, refined for design.

PowerLift for statements

TYPETR PowerLift is a bold typeface experiment that pushes the boundaries of extreme weight and visual impact. Designed to explore the limits of black letterforms, PowerLift amplifies contrast, density, and spatial tension to create a typeface that commands attention. With its unapologetically heavy strokes and sculptural presence, PowerLift isn't just a font—it’s a statement of typographic power, ideal for headlines, posters, and design work that demands a dramatic visual voice.

Proforma Classic — An original Landmark

Originally released by Font Bureau in the early 1990s, Proforma was a groundbreaking typeface that set new standards for digital text typography. Designed for clarity and efficiency in long-form reading, it combines classical proportions with a contemporary sensibility, balancing robust structure and subtle detail. Its carefully engineered spacing and intelligent use of contrast made it a favorite among typographers for editorial, corporate, and institutional use. Proforma remains a milestone in type design—timeless, readable, and remarkably forward-thinking.

Upgrade your typography

TYPETR Upgrade is a refined sans-serif built on the subtle translation contrast of a broad-nib pencil, bringing warmth and rhythm to a clean, modern structure. Its extended glyph set supports a wide range of Latin-based languages, while advanced OpenType features—such as alternate forms, small caps, and multiple numeral styles (12345 12345 12345 12345)—offer designers precise typographic control. Upgrade clarity with character.

Productus

TYPETR Productus is a clean, versatile sans-serif with roots in modernist design, shaped for clarity without losing personality. Its straightforward forms are built for function, but small details give it a human touch—making it equally at home in serious text and bold display settings.

Bitcount Mono

TYPETR Bitcount is a 5x7 bitmap typeface initiated in 1977, designed within the strict confines of early digital grids. What sets Bitcount apart is its innovative use of contrast, even within such a minimal pixel matrix, to preserve essential typographic details and ensure clear differentiation between letterforms.
