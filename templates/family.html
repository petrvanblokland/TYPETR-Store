Styles & axes

Titel van de Eerste Kolom

Gerrit Noordzij’s cube is based on a merger of expansion contrast (sharp-nib pencil) and translation contrast (broad-nib pencil). The type of contrast used in Upgrade is based on translation, with little difference between vertical thick and horizontal thin strokes. The animation (based on software by Just van Rossum’s Daily Drawbot) shows where the contrast principle of Upgrade is located in Noordzij’s cube. Upgrade styles shown in this example are Light, Book, Regular, Semibold, and Black.
