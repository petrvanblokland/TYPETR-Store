Upgrade your typography

TYPETR Upgrade is a refined sans-serif built on the subtle translation contrast of a broad-nib pencil, bringing warmth and rhythm to a clean, modern structure. Its extended glyph set supports a wide range of Latin-based languages, while advanced OpenType features—such as alternate forms, small caps, and multiple numeral styles—offer designers precise typographic control. Upgrade clarity with character.
